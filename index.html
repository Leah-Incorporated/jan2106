Code for DC

I am a co-captain of Code for DC, a local volunteer group of civic hackers. We are the DC brigade of Code for America, and we work together to solve local issues, improve city services, and help people engage with DC. Learn more.

You don't need to be a coder to join. We use technology in our projects, but we need everyone who can help make a digital service or website better: designers, communications experts, digital strategists, people who care about ux, community organizers, developers, and more.
